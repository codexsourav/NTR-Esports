Privacy Policy

Welcome to NTR eSports

At NTR Esports, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and share your personal information in connection with our BGMI tournaments.

1.Our Privacy Policy

What Information We Collect

• Contact information: Your name, email address, phone number, and other contact details.
• Payment information: Your billing address, or other payment information.
• Gaming information: Your BGMI username, game stats, and other gaming-related information.
• Communication information: Records of any communication between you and NTR Esports, including emails and chat logs.

How We Collect Information

We collect information directly from you when you sign up for our tournaments, make payments, and communicate with us. We may also collect information from third-party sources such as payment processors.

How We Use Information

• Register you for our tournaments and process payments.
• Communicate with you about our tournaments and related services.
• Improve our tournaments and services.
• Comply with legal obligations.
We may also use your information to send you marketing emails or other promotional materials, but only if you have explicitly opted in to receive such communications.

How We Share Information

We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who assist us in providing our tournaments and related services. We may also share your information if required by law or if we believe such disclosure is necessary to protect our legal rights or the rights of others.

Your Rights

You have the right to access or correct your personal information that we hold. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.
