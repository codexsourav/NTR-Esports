Welcome to NTR eSports

NTR Esports is a professional esports organization that was founded in 2020 and has since established itself as a strong contender in the esports scene.

1.Our Refund policy

Refunds for Non-Participation

If a player is unable to participate in a BGMI tournament due to unforeseen circumstances such as illness or injury, they may request a refund of their entry fee. The refund request must be made before the start of the tournament, and supporting documentation may be required.

Refunds for Cancellation

If NTR Esports cancels a BGMI tournament for any reason, all registered players will receive a full refund of their entry fee.

Refunds for Disqualification

If a player is disqualified from a BGMI tournament for violating the code of conduct or any other rules, they will not be entitled to a refund of their entry fee.

Refund Processing

Refunds will be processed within 30 days of the request being received, and will be issued in the same form as the original payment. NTR Esports is not responsible for any fees or charges incurred by players as a result of the refund.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are only available to players who have paid the entry fee for the tournament in question. Any fees or charges related to travel, accommodation, or other expenses incurred by the player are the responsibility of the player and are not eligible for a refund.

Changes to Terms and Conditions

NTR Esports may modify its terms and conditions at any time, with or without prior notice to players.

At The End

By participating in an NTR Esports BGMI tournament, players agree to abide by this refund policy. NTR Esports reserves the right to modify this policy at any time, with or without prior notice to players. If you have any questions or concerns regarding this refund policy, please contact NTR Esports customer support.
