About Us

NTR Esports is a professional esports organization that was founded in 2020 and has since established itself as a strong contender in the esports scene. The organization is dedicated to creating a competitive environment for its players and helping them reach their full potential, with a particular focus on mobile gaming such as Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI). NTR Esports has achieved several notable accomplishments in esports, placing highly in various tournaments and leagues and earning a strong reputation in the community. In addition to their competitive success, NTR Esports is committed to creating a positive impact in the esports industry, providing a platform for talented gamers to pursue their dreams of becoming professional esports athletes and promoting fair play and sportsmanship. Overall, NTR Esports is a well-respected and accomplished organization with a commitment to excellence, community engagement, and innovation in esports. Their involvement in a wide range of popular esports titles, including BGMI, has cemented their status as a major player in the industry.
